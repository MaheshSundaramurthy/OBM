How Health Check works:

Welcome in OBM Health check System

This is the right place after having installed OBM or after an upgrade.

Pre-conditions :

  • a network access to your OBM servers
  • a domain created
  • nothing is required in your browser
  • nothing already installed on your OBM server is required

How to use it :

First, click on "Start Checking OBM". A list of available checks will be returned and launched automatically. For example, system service status will be tested, access to service will be tested, Database integrity will be checked...and many more.
Most of the time, you will have a success returned. But in case of an error returned, a description of a reason why the error occurs will be displayed to you in order to help you to fix it.

Just click on "Start" and let the powerful OBM health check guide you !
