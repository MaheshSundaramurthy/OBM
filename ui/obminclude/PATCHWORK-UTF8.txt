=======================
Patchwork\Utf8 for PHP
=======================

The obminclude/Normalizer.php file and obminclude/Patchwork directory and its
contents are part of Patchwork\Utf8, an "Extensive, portable and performant
handling of UTF-8 and grapheme clusters for PHP" according to the home page.

It can be found here: https://github.com/nicolas-grekas/Patchwork-UTF8
